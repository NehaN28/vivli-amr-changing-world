# Data and attribution notice

The MIT licence in this repository applies to the original software and
documentation. It does not relicense source datasets or override their terms.

The workflow uses data from:

- the ATLAS antimicrobial-resistance dataset accessed through Vivli;
- the Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project (ACLED);
- FAOSTAT temperature and livestock datasets;
- World Organisation for Animal Health (WOAH) animal antimicrobial-use reports;
- the Global AMR R&D Hub investment dataset.

Users must obtain restricted source data through the relevant provider and
comply with every source's citation, attribution, access and reuse conditions.
The repository does not distribute raw or isolate-level ATLAS data.

The packaged dashboard tables are aggregated and technically disclosure
checked. Public redistribution of ATLAS-derived aggregates remains conditional
on confirmation under the applicable Vivli data-use agreement.
